Water Balance and Budgets for Watersheds

The National Hydrography Dataset is made up of all the water features in the United States, as defined by the U.S. Geological Survey and is the most commonly used dataset for water features. Although it will soon be replaced by NHDPlus High Resolution which has not been completed for the entore U.S. yet.

Today, we will be walking through how to calculate a water budget for a watershed using R. Follow the slides to get started.
