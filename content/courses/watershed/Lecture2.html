Hydrology (Water Science)

  • Study of the occurrence, circulation, and distribution of the water of the earth and its atmosphere – The global hydrologic cycle: — Terrestrial — Oceanic — Atmospheric – The land phase of the hydrologic cycle: — Water on and under the earth’s surface — Physical, chemical, biological processes
